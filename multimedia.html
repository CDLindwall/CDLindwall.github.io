Creative writing doesn't necessarily have to be read through a book. Some of the most inspiring and powerful creative writing comes in the form of written poetry. This video of Rudy Francisco's "Love Poem" shows that writing can be a performance, as well. While I have never personally written poetry, hearing it outloud brings new rhythm and life to language that I think is important to carry into my everyday writing -- professional and otherwise.
